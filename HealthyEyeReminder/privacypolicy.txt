Privacy Policy for Healthy Eye Reminder Extension
Effective Date: 10-Dec-2024

Healthy Eye Reminder is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Data Collection
Healthy Eye Reminder collects the following data to provide its functionality:

Work and Rest Intervals: The durations you configure for work and rest periods.
Extension Preferences: Your preferences for reminder settings.
No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected.

2. Data Usage
The data collected is used solely to:

Notify you to take breaks based on your configured schedule.
Customize reminders and enforce your preferred settings.
We do not sell, share, or use your data for any purpose other than providing and improving the extension's functionality.

3. Data Storage
All data collected by the extension is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit or store your data on external servers or third-party systems.

4. Permissions
The extension requires certain permissions to function correctly, including:

Storage Permission: To store your preferences and configurations locally.
Notification Permission: To send reminders for breaks.
These permissions are used strictly to enable core functionality.

5. Data Sharing
We do not share your data with third parties under any circumstances.

6. User Rights
You have full control over the data stored by the extension. You can:

Edit your work and rest intervals, and other preferences, at any time.
Uninstall the extension, which will delete all locally stored data.
7. Changes to this Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated by updating the Effective Date at the top of this policy.
